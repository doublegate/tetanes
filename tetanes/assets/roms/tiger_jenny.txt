Tiger Jenny
By Ludosity

Tiger Jenny by Ludosity is a NES game set in the same universe as “Ittle Dew” it
takes place a thousand years before the events of that game. Battle your way
through the forests to seek vengeance on the Turnip Witch who dwells in her
castle.
